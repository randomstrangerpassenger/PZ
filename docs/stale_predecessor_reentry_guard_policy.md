# Stale / Predecessor Reentry Guard Policy

Historical, diagnostic, fixture, quarantine, predecessor, rollback, monolith, stale bridge, and package-fallback artifacts are denied from current Registry surfaces by default.

The guard scans the live census and producer/consumer/readpoint references across source, rendered, exporter, runtime, package, required-manifest, and documentation claim surfaces. Renaming does not authorize reentry; existing archives are inspected by content hashes as well as names. Unknown current-looking paths fail closed.

Negated, quoted, and explicitly role-qualified historical statements remain legal. Classification never authorizes deletion of history or quarantine evidence.
